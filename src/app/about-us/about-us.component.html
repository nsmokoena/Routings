About US

Accommodation in a forest in Mpumalanga / Nomtees's resort in Mpumalanga offer a different kind of holiday,
with accommodation normally at the same height as the forest canopy, often in eco-friendly treehouses,
giving wonderful views of the surrounding areas.
Accommodation ranges from rustic to luxury.
Forest Lodges in Mpumalanga offer the added attraction of game viewing at nearby game reserves, such as the Kruger National Park
